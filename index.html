About Yellow Bricks Bahrain

Yellow Bricks Co WLL (also searched as Yellowbricks and Yellow Bricks Bahrain) is a trusted interior design and turnkey fit-out contractor based in Bahrain. We design and build home interiors, commercial spaces, home cinemas, and custom wooden doors across Manama, Muharraq, Riffa, Saar, Seef, Juffair, Amwaj, and all Bahrain.
