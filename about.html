About Yellow Bricks

Yellow Bricks Co. WLL is a Bahrain-based interior design firm dedicated to creating modern living and office spaces that inspire and impress. With a passionate team of designers and architects, we transform ideas into reality by blending creativity, functionality, and innovation. Our work spans from concept to completion, ensuring every detail reflects the unique needs and personality of our clients. Whether it's a residential project or a corporate environment, we deliver designs that are both stylish and practical. By combining craftsmanship with forward-thinking solutions, we create spaces that leave a lasting impression. At Yellow Bricks Co., every project is a journey toward redefining the way people live and work.

Mission & Vision

At Yellow Bricks, our mission and vision is to craft unforgettable experiences for every client we serve. We strive to be Bahrain's go-to interior design company — known for creativity, quality, and trust — delivering spaces that inspire, impress, and endure.
