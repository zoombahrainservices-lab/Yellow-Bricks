Frequently Asked Questions - Commercial Fit-Out Bahrain

What areas in Bahrain do you provide commercial fit-out services?

We provide complete commercial fit-out services across Bahrain including Manama CBD, Seef District, Juffair, Riffa, Muharraq, Sitra Industrial Area, and all major commercial zones. Serving offices, retail, restaurants, and showrooms nationwide.

How much does commercial fit-out cost in Bahrain?

Commercial fit-out costs in Bahrain: Office (BD 30-80 per sqm), Retail (BD 40-100 per sqm), Restaurant (BD 60-150 per sqm). Includes partitions, flooring, MEP, joinery, ceiling. Custom quote based on scope and finishes.

Do you handle office fit-out in Manama business district?

Yes! We specialize in office fit-out across Manama including Financial Harbour, Diplomatic Area, Seef business district, and Sanabis. Full turnkey service: design, partitions, MEP, furniture, IT cabling, approvals, and handover.

How long does commercial fit-out take in Bahrain?

Timeline depends on project size: Small office (200 sqm): 4-6 weeks. Medium retail (500 sqm): 8-12 weeks. Large restaurant/showroom (1000 sqm): 12-20 weeks. Includes design, approvals, construction, and handover.

Do you handle municipality approvals for commercial projects in Bahrain?

Yes, we manage all approvals including Civil Defence, Municipality, EWA, and landlord permissions. Our team handles full documentation, submissions, and coordination with authorities across Bahrain for hassle-free project delivery.

Do you provide fit-out for retail shops in Bahrain malls?

Absolutely! We have extensive experience with retail fit-out in City Centre Bahrain, Seef Mall, Avenues Mall, Dana Mall, and independent retail locations. Full compliance with mall guidelines and fast-track execution available.
