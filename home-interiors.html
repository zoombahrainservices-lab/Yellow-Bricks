Frequently Asked Questions

What areas in Bahrain do you serve for home interior design?

We provide complete home interior design and fit-out services across all Bahrain including Manama, Muharraq, Riffa, Isa Town, Hamad Town, Saar, Seef, Juffair, Amwaj Islands, Budaiya, and Janabiyah. We offer free site visits anywhere in Bahrain.

How much does home interior design cost in Bahrain?

Interior design costs in Bahrain vary based on project scope, materials, and finishes. We offer flexible packages for apartments starting from BD 5,000 and villas from BD 15,000. Contact us for a free consultation and customized quote tailored to your budget.

How long does a home interior project take in Bahrain?

Timeline depends on project size. Typically, apartment interiors take 4-8 weeks, while villa projects take 8-16 weeks from design to completion. We provide detailed timelines during consultation and ensure timely delivery.

Do you provide turnkey interior solutions in Bahrain?

Yes! We offer complete turnkey interior solutions covering design, approvals, civil work, joinery, MEP, furnishing, and final handover. You get a single point of contact for your entire project across Bahrain.

Do you handle interior design for modern apartments in Manama?

Absolutely! We specialize in modern apartment interiors in Manama, Seef, Juffair, and across Bahrain. We understand space optimization for apartments and create functional, stylish designs that maximize every square foot.

Do you provide warranty on interior work in Bahrain?

Yes, we provide comprehensive warranty on all our interior work. Structural work comes with 2-year warranty, joinery with 1-year warranty, and we offer ongoing maintenance support across Bahrain.
