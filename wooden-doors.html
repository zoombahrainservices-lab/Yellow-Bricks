Solid Wooden Doors

Crafted from a single piece of high-quality timber, Solid Wooden Doors offer exceptional strength, sound insulation, and timeless beauty. Perfect for main entrances or rooms where elegance and durability matter most.

Flush Doors

Sleek and modern, Flush Doors feature a smooth, flat surface ideal for contemporary interiors. They are lightweight, easy to maintain, and available in various finishes to complement your décor seamlessly.

Grooved Doors

Add a touch of texture and style with Grooved Wooden Doors. Characterized by fine lines and patterns carved into the surface, these doors bring visual depth and modern sophistication to any space.

Carved Doors

A masterpiece of craftsmanship, Carved Wooden Doors showcase intricate detailing and artistic patterns. Ideal for grand entrances and statement interiors, these doors blend tradition with luxury for a truly elegant finish.

Frequently Asked Questions - Wooden Doors Bahrain

What areas in Bahrain do you supply wooden doors?

We supply and install custom wooden doors across all Bahrain including Manama, Muharraq, Riffa, Isa Town, Hamad Town, Saar, and surrounding areas. Free site measurement and consultation available.

How much do wooden doors cost in Bahrain?

Wooden door prices in Bahrain vary by type: Flush doors (BD 80-150), Solid wood doors (BD 200-500), Grooved doors (BD 150-300), Carved doors (BD 300-800). Prices include basic installation. Contact us for detailed quotation.

Do you provide fire-rated wooden doors in Bahrain?

Yes! We supply fire-rated wooden doors compliant with Bahrain building codes. Available in 30-minute, 60-minute, and 90-minute fire ratings for commercial and residential projects across Bahrain.

Can you custom make wooden doors for villas in Bahrain?

Absolutely! We specialize in custom wooden door manufacturing for villas, apartments, and commercial spaces across Bahrain. Choose from various woods (oak, walnut, teak), finishes, and designs. Made-to-measure service available.

How long does wooden door installation take in Bahrain?

Standard doors: 2-3 weeks (including manufacturing and installation). Custom carved doors: 4-6 weeks. Fire-rated doors: 3-4 weeks. Timeline includes site measurement, manufacturing, delivery, and professional installation.

Do you provide warranty on wooden doors in Bahrain?

Yes, all our wooden doors come with manufacturer warranty: 2 years on solid wood doors, 1 year on flush/grooved doors, and 1 year on installation workmanship. Maintenance support available across Bahrain.
